About Me

My background

I graduated from the University of North Texas in 2015 with a B.S. in Digital Retailing and Merchandising, Minor in Business Foundations, and Minor in Marketing. I interned at Michaels Crafts, Sally Beauty, and Signet Jewellers, in various merchandising and e-commerce roles. I have since worked at Signet Jewellers as an E-Commerce Promotions Specialist, at Pier 1 Imports as the Manager of Email and SMS, and most recently at Feedonomics as an Enterprise Feed Manager. I am currently studying at The University of Denver to be a Full Stack Developer with anticipated completion in December 2020.

Who I Am

I am a 27 year old woman living in Arvada, Colorado. I live with my partner, Patrick, and our four pets: Benjamin, Cece, Ollie, and Nora. I am a professional photographer and also a problem-solving nerd. I love to grow plants, play video games, watch sports (Go Nuggets!), and keep up with politics. I thrive in the outdoors so I will frequently be found in the mountains hiking, camping, or lounging in my hammock. I love to read and always have at least 4 books open at any given time, typically including at least one Kurt Vonnegut novel.
